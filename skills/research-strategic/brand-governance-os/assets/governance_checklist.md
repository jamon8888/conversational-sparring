# Brand Governance Checklist

## 1. Visual Identity
- [ ] **Logo Usage**: Correct clear space, minimum size, and color variation?
- [ ] **Color Palette**: Only approved primary/secondary colors used?
- [ ] **Typography**: Correct fonts (Poppins/Lora) and hierarchy?
- [ ] **Imagery**: High-resolution, on-brand style, diverse representation?

## 2. Messaging & Voice
- [ ] **Tone Check**: Is it [Attribute 1] and [Attribute 2]?
- [ ] **Product Naming**: Are all product names capitalized and spelled correctly?
- [ ] **Claims**: Are all stats/claims backed by linked evidence?
- [ ] **Grammar**: Adheres to style guide (e.g., Oxford comma, sentence case)?

## 3. Compliance & Legal
- [ ] **Trademarks**: Proper TM/Â® symbols on first mention?
- [ ] **Disclaimers**: Required legal text included (e.g., "Terms apply")?
- [ ] **Accessibility**: Alt text for images, high contrast, legible font sizes?
- [ ] **GDPR/Privacy**: Consent language included if collecting data?

## 4. Approval Routing
- [ ] **Stakeholder Review**: Has [Product/Legal/Exec] signed off?
- [ ] **Regional Check**: Validated for [Region] cultural nuance?
