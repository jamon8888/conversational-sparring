# Brand Voice Matrix

## Core Attributes

| Attribute | We Are... | We Are NOT... | Example Phrase |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Confident** | Expert, Assured, Clear | Arrogant, Pushy, Loud | "The data shows X." (Not: "We are the best.") |
| **Empathetic** | Understanding, Human, Warm | Soft, Apologetic, Informal | "We know this is hard." (Not: "Sorry about that.") |
| **Visionary** | Forward-looking, Inspiring | Unrealistic, Vague, Hype-y | "Build the future." (Not: "Disrupt the paradigm.") |
| **Precise** | Accurate, Specific, Data-driven | Pedantic, Dry, Complex | "30% faster." (Not: "Significantly optimized.") |

## Vocabulary Guardrails

### Preferred Terms
- **Partner** (not Vendor)
- **Challenge** (not Problem)
- **Insight** (not Data point)
- **Team** (not Staff)

### Banned Words
- **Synergy**
- **Disrupt**
- **Best-in-class**
- **Cutting-edge**

## Formatting Rules
- **Headings**: Sentence case (e.g., "Start your journey").
- **Oxford Comma**: Always use it.
- **Numbers**: Spell out 1-9, use digits for 10+.
- **Dates**: Month Day, Year (e.g., January 1, 2024).
