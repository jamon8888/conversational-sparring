# Comparative Analyzer

Deep competitive intelligence and strategic positioning analysis.

## When to Use

- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Positioning analysis
- SWOT analysis
- Differentiation strategy
- Competitive benchmarking
- Market positioning maps

## Core Capabilities

### Competitor Profiling

- Company overview and history
- Product/service offerings
- Pricing strategies
- Go-to-market approach
- Strengths and weaknesses

### Positioning Analysis

- Positioning maps (2x2 matrices)
- Value proposition comparison
- Messaging analysis
- Brand differentiation
- Target customer overlap

### SWOT Analysis

**For Each Competitor:**

- **Strengths**: Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Internal disadvantages
- **Opportunities**: External favorable factors
- **Threats**: External unfavorable factors

## Workflows

### Workflow 1: Competitive Intelligence

1. Identify competitors (direct, indirect, emerging)
2. Profile top 5-10 competitors
3. Analyze positioning and messaging
4. Create competitive matrix
5. Identify gaps and opportunities

### Workflow 2: Differentiation Strategy

1. Map competitive landscape
2. Identify positioning clusters
3. Find white space opportunities
4. Define unique value proposition
5. Validate with customer insights

## Quality Standards

- **Coverage**: Minimum 5 competitors profiled
- **Depth**: SWOT for each competitor
- **Positioning**: Visual positioning map
- **Sources**: Mix of public filings, press releases, customer reviews
- **Actionability**: Clear differentiation recommendations

## Deliverables

- Competitive intelligence report
- Positioning map (visual)
- SWOT analysis matrix
- Differentiation strategy brief
- Competitive monitoring plan
