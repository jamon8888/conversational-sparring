# Landing Page Copy Mastery

## Overview
Write high-converting landing pages that turn visitors into leads and customers. This skill helps solo entrepreneurs create persuasive copy for product pages, lead capture pages, and sales pages without a copywriter.

---

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating lead magnet opt-in pages
- Writing product or service pages
- Building webinar registration pages
- Designing sales pages for offers
- Creating "About" or "Contact" pages
- Optimizing existing low-converting pages

---

## Core Framework: AIDA

Every high-converting landing page follows this psychology:

### **A** - Attention (Hook)
**Goal:** Stop them from bouncing in 3 seconds

**Elements:**
- Compelling headline
- Supporting subheadline
- Eye-catching visual
- Above-the-fold clarity

---

### **I** - Interest (Build curiosity)
**Goal:** Make them want to learn more

**Elements:**
- Problem identification
- Relatable scenario
- Intriguing promise
- Social proof tease

---

### **D** - Desire (Show transformation)
**Goal:** Make them want what you're offering

**Elements:**
- Benefits (not just features)
- Social proof (testimonials, logos)
- Before/after transformation
- Overcome objections

---

### **A** - Action (Clear CTA)
**Goal:** Make it easy to convert

**Elements:**
- Prominent CTA button
- Remove friction
- Create urgency
- Repeat CTA multiple times

## Landing Page Structure

### Above-the-Fold (First screen - CRITICAL)

**Headline formula:**
```
[Benefit] for [Target Audience] without [Pain Point]

Examples:
"Get 10x More Leads Without Spending on Ads"
"Close More Deals Without Wasting Time on Bad Fits"
"Build Your Email List Without Complex Tech"
```

**Subheadline formula:**
```
[How it works] + [Timeframe/Ease] + [Proof]

Examples:
"Our proven framework helps B2B founders generate 100+ qualified leads per month in just 15 minutes per day. Over 500 companies use it."
```

**Above-the-fold checklist:**
- [ ] Clear headline (10 words or less)
- [ ] Supporting subheadline (15-25 words)
- [ ] Primary CTA button (contrasting color)
- [ ] Hero image/video (shows product or outcome)
- [ ] Trust indicator (# of users, rating, logo)

## Conversion Optimization Tactics

### 1. Remove Friction

**Bad form:**
```
[ ] First name
[ ] Last name
[ ] Email
[ ] Phone
[ ] Company
[ ] Role
[ ] Company size
[ ] How did you hear about us?
```

**Good form (for lead magnet):**
```
[ ] Email
[Big button: Send Me the Guide]
```

**Rule:** Only ask for what you NEED. More fields = lower conversion.

---

### 2. Create Urgency

**Techniques:**
```

üë• Scarcity: "Only 10 spots left"
```

### 5. Video vs. Text

**Video (use when):**
- Explaining complex product
- Building personal connection
- Showing software demo
- Founder-led sales

**Video best practices:**
- 2-3 minutes max
- Auto-play (muted) or click-to-play
- Captions (80% watch without sound)
- Thumbnail with face + text overlay

**Text (use when):**
- Simple offer (e.g., free ebook)
- SEO-focused page
- Fast-loading priority
- Mobile-first audience

## Mobile Optimization

**60%+ of traffic is mobile** - optimize for small screens:

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Headline readable (large font)
- [ ] CTA button thumb-friendly (44px+ height)

**Test these elements:**

- Benefit-focused vs. curiosity-driven

- Color (orange vs. green vs. red)

- Product shot vs. person using product

- Email only vs. name + email

**Pricing:**
- Monthly vs. annual vs. one-time

**Run tests for 2-4 weeks** or 1,000+ visitors minimum.

## Landing Page Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators

**Conversion Rate:**
- **Formula:** Conversions / Visitors √ó 100
- **Targets:**
  - Lead magnet page: 30-50%
  - Webinar registration: 20-40%
  - Product page: 2-5%
  - High-ticket service: 5-15%

**Bounce Rate:**
- **Formula:** Single-page visits / Total visits √ó 100
- **Target:** <40%
- **High bounce means:** Headline doesn't match ad, slow load time, poor design

**Time on Page:**
- **Target:** 2-5 minutes (long-form sales pages)
- **Low time means:** Not reading (need better hook)

**Scroll Depth:**
- **Target:** 75%+ scroll to bottom
- **Track:** Heatmaps (Hotjar, Crazy Egg)

## Common Landing Page Mistakes

### ‚ùå Unclear value proposition
**Problem:** Visitor doesn't understand what you offer
**Fix:** Clear headline + subheadline above fold

### ‚ùå Too many CTAs
**Problem:** Decision paralysis
**Fix:** One primary action per page

### ‚ùå Weak headline
**Problem:** Visitor bounces in 3 seconds
**Fix:** Lead with biggest benefit

### ‚ùå No social proof
**Problem:** Trust issues
**Fix:** Add testimonials, logos, stats

### ‚ùå Long forms
**Problem:** Low conversion
**Fix:** Only ask for what you need

### ‚ùå Slow load time
**Problem:** 40% bounce if >3 seconds
**Fix:** Compress images, minimize code

### ‚ùå Generic copy
**Problem:** Doesn't resonate
**Fix:** Speak to specific pain points

## Pro Tips

### Steal from Top Performers
- Analyze competitor landing pages
- Save swipe file of high-converting pages
- Note: Headlines, CTAs, social proof placement

### Use Heatmaps
Tools like Hotjar show:
- Where people click
- How far they scroll
- Where they get stuck

### Write Headlines Last
- Write the body copy first
- Then distill the main benefit into headline
- Easier than starting with headline

### Cut Ruthlessly
- Every word should have a purpose
- If it doesn't help convert, delete it
- Shorter > longer (with exceptions for complex offers)

### Use Specificity
```
‚ùå "Many customers"
‚úÖ "1,247 customers"

‚ùå "Fast results"
‚úÖ "Results in 30 days"

‚ùå "High reply rates"
‚úÖ "15% average reply rate"
```

Specific = believable.
