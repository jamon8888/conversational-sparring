# Content Distribution

## Quick Start

This skill helps you distribute content across channels. For complete frameworks, see the [Complete Guide](references/guide.md).

## Execution Checklist

### Distributing Content

- [ ] Publish on owned channels (blog, email, social)
- [ ] Share in communities (Reddit, forums, Slack groups)
- [ ] Syndicate to platforms (Medium, LinkedIn, Dev.to)
- [ ] Reach out to mentioned people/brands
- [ ] Partner with complementary brands
- [ ] Run paid promotion (ads, sponsored posts)
- [ ] Pitch to media/publications
- [ ] Repurpose into multiple formats
- [ ] Track traffic sources

## Key Frameworks

- **POE Model (Paid-Owned-Earned)**: Balance across paid ads, owned channels, and earned media
- **Content Funnel**: Match distribution to buyer journey stage (awareness, consideration, decision)
- **80/20 Distribution Rule**: Spend 20% time creating, 80% promoting
- **Channel-Content Fit**: Align content format with channel strengths

## Distribution Channels

- **Owned**: Blog, email list, social profiles
- **Earned**: PR, guest posts, backlinks, shares
- **Paid**: Ads, sponsored content, influencer partnerships
- **Community**: Reddit, forums, Slack, Discord

## Quick Wins

- Email list first (highest engagement)
- Tag people mentioned in content
- Post in 3-5 relevant communities
- Run small ad test ($50-100 to best content)
