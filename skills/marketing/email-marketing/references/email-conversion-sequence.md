# Email Conversion Sequence Framework

## Overview
Every successful email marketing strategy follows this progression:

```
Lead Magnet → Welcome Series → Regular Newsletters → Nurture Sequences → Sales Campaigns
```

## Component Breakdown

### 1. Lead Magnet
**Purpose**: Capture email addresses with high-value offer
**Examples**: 
- Ebook/Guide
- Template/Toolkit
- Checklist
- Webinar

**Best Practices**:
- Solve one specific problem
- Deliver immediately
- Require minimal time investment
- Align with your core offer

### 2. Welcome Series (3-5 emails)
**Purpose**: Build trust and set expectations

**Sequence**:
```
Email 1 (Day 0): Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself
Email 2 (Day 2): Your story / why you're credible
Email 3 (Day 4): Your best content / resources
Email 4 (Day 7): Social proof / case studies
Email 5 (Day 10): Soft pitch / next step
```

### 3. Regular Newsletters
**Purpose**: Stay top-of-mind, provide value, drive traffic
**Cadence**: Weekly (same day/time)
**Mix**: 80% education, 20% promotion

### 4. Nurture Sequences
**Purpose**: Move leads through funnel based on behavior

**Types**:
- **Education nurture**: Teaching your methodology
- **Product nurture**: Highlighting features/benefits
- **Re-engagement**: Winning back inactive subscribers
- **Abandoned cart**: Recovering lost sales

### 5. Sales Campaigns
**Purpose**: Drive conversions for launches/promotions

**Structure (5-7 emails over 7-10 days)**:
```
Email 1: Announce (what's coming)
Email 2: Educate (why it matters)
Email 3: Social proof (who's succeeded)
Email 4: Overcome objections (FAQs)
Email 5: Urgency (limited time/spots)
Email 6: Last chance
Email 7 (optional): Final warning
```

## Success Metrics
- **Welcome Series**: 40-50% open rate
- **Newsletters**: 20-30% open rate
- **Nurture**: 15-25% click-through rate
- **Sales**: 5-10% conversion rate
