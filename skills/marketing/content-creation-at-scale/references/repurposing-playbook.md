# Content Repurposing Playbook

## The Content Pyramid

```
        1 Pillar Content (Blog Post)
               ↓
    ┌──────────┴──────────┐
    ↓                     ↓
5 Social Posts      3 Email Updates
    ↓                     ↓
10 Quotes          1 Video Script
```

## Repurposing Workflows

### Blog Post → Multi-Platform
1. **Extract Key Points** (5-10 main ideas)
2. **Create Twitter Thread** (1 point per tweet)
3. **Write LinkedIn Post** (summary + 3 takeaways)
4. **Generate Instagram Captions** (visual quotes)
5. **Create Email Newsletter** (summary + link)
6. **Develop Video Script** (talking head or slides)

### Video → Multi-Format
1. **Transcribe Video** (auto or manual)
2. **Create Blog Post** (edit transcript)
3. **Pull Quotes** (10+ shareable quotes)
4. **Generate Audiogram** (audio + waveform visual)
5. **Create Clips** (short highlights)

### Podcast → Content Series
1. **Transcribe Episode**
2. **Create Show Notes** (summary + timestamps)
3. **Extract Quotes** (guest insights)
4. **Write Blog Post** (key topics expanded)
5. **Create Social Posts** (teaser clips)

## Time Savings

From 1 blog post (4 hours to create):
- 5 Twitter threads → 10 min
- 3 LinkedIn posts → 15 min
- 10 Quote cards → 20 min
- 1 Email newsletter → 10 min
- Total repurposing time: ~1 hour
- Total content pieces: 20+
