# Platform-Specific Guidelines

## Twitter/X

### Best Practices
- **Character Limit**: 280 chars
- **Optimal Length**: 71-100 chars (highest engagement)
- **Hashtags**: 1-2 max
- **Images**: 1200x675px (16:9 ratio)
- **Best Times**: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm

### Engagement Tactics
- Ask questions
- Use threads for long content
- Leverage trending topics
- Reply to comments quickly

## LinkedIn

### Best Practices
- **Character Limit**: 3,000 chars
- **Optimal Length**: 1,200-1,500 chars
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 max
- **Images**: 1200x627px
- **Best Times**: 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm

### Engagement Tactics
- Professional tone
- Share data and insights
- Use line breaks for readability
- Engage with comments

## Instagram

### Best Practices
- **Caption Limit**: 2,200 chars
- **Optimal Length**: 138-150 chars
- **Hashtags**: 5-10 (up to 30)
- **Images**: 1080x1080px (square)
- **Best Times**: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm

### Engagement Tactics
- Visual storytelling
- Carousel posts
- Reels for reach
- Stories for engagement
