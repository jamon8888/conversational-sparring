# Content Formats Guide

## Long-Form Content

### Blog Posts
- **Length**: 1,500-2,500 words
- **Structure**: Title, intro, H2 sections, conclusion
- **SEO**: Keywords, meta description, internal links
- **Best For**: Thought leadership, tutorials, guides

### White Papers
- **Length**: 10+ pages
- **Structure**: Executive summary, problem, solution, data, conclusion
- **Format**: PDF with professional design
- **Best For**: B2B, enterprise sales, authority building

## Short-Form Content

### Twitter Threads
- **Length**: 5-10 tweets
- **Structure**: Hook → Points → CTA
- **Format**: Numbered tweets with emoji
- **Best For**: Breaking down complex topics

### LinkedIn Posts
- **Length**: 1,200-1,500 chars
- **Structure**: Hook → Context → Insights → CTA
- **Format**: Short paragraphs with line breaks
- **Best For**: Professional insights, career content

## Video Content

### YouTube Videos
- **Length**: 8-15 minutes (optimal)
- **Structure**: Hook → Content → CTA
- **Format**: HD 1080p or 4K
- **Best For**: Tutorials, interviews, vlogs

### Short-Form Video
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds
- **Structure**: Hook in 3 seconds → Value → CTA
- **Format**: Vertical 9:16
- **Best For**: Tips, quick wins, entertainment
