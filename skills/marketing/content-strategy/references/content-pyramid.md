# Content Pyramid Framework

## Overview
Turn one pillar piece into 10+ derivative content assets to maximize ROI on content creation.

## The Pyramid Structure

```
         [1 Pillar Content]
         (Blog post, video, podcast)
                  ↓
    [3-5 Medium-Form Pieces]
    (LinkedIn articles, email series)
                  ↓
      [10-15 Micro-Content]
      (Social posts, quotes, threads)
                  ↓
        [Evergreen Assets]
        (Lead magnets, templates)
```

## Implementation Process

### Step 1: Create Pillar Content
**Formats**:
- Long-form blog post (2,000+ words)
- Podcast episode (30-45 min)
- Video tutorial (15-20 min)
- In-depth guide or case study

**Requirements**:
- Comprehensive coverage of topic
- Original research or insights
- Actionable frameworks
- SEO-optimized (for blog)

### Step 2: Extract Key Insights
**Goal**: Identify 10-15 standalone ideas

**Method**:
- Pull out key statistics
- Extract quotable insights
- Identify tactical tips
- Note framework components

### Step 3: Create Medium-Form Pieces
**Formats**:
- 3-5 LinkedIn articles (600-1,000 words)
- Email series (5-7 emails)
- Twitter/X threads (5-10 tweets)

**Approach**: Deep dive into specific subtopics

### Step 4: Generate Micro-Content
**Formats**:
- 15-20 social posts
- Quote graphics
- Short video clips
- Carousel posts

**Distribution**: Spread over 4-6 weeks

### Step 5: Package as Evergreen Asset
**Formats**:
- Downloadable template
- Checklist
- Toolkit
- Mini-course

## Example Breakdown

**Pillar**: "Complete Guide to Sales Prospecting" (3,000-word blog)

**Derivatives**:
- 5 LinkedIn articles (600 words each on subtopics)
- 15 LinkedIn posts (one per key insight)
- 3 Twitter threads (tactical tips)
- 1 email series (5-part nurture)
- 1 lead magnet (prospecting template)
- 1 webinar (deep dive)

**Result**: 1 piece of research → 25+ content assets

## Distribution Timeline

```
Day 1: Publish pillar content (blog post)
Day 2-3: Share 3 LinkedIn posts highlighting key insights
Day 4: Twitter thread with tactical tips
Day 5: Email newsletter featuring the post
Week 2: LinkedIn article diving deeper into one subtopic
Week 3: Create lead magnet (downloadable template)
Week 4: Webinar expanding on the topic
```

## Benefits
- **Efficiency**: 4-6 weeks of content from 1 research session
- **Consistency**: Never run out of content ideas
- **SEO**: Multiple entry points to your content
- **Reach**: Different formats for different audiences
