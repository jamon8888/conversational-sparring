# Multi-Language Content

## Quick Start

Localize content for global markets with AI translation, cultural adaptation, and multi-language SEO. Supports 10+ major languages with brand voice preservation and glossary management.

(references/multi-language-guide.md)

## Execution Checklist

### Localize Your Content

- [ ] Select target languages and regions
- [ ] Translate content (AI or human)
- [ ] Adapt for cultural context (examples, references, currency)
- [ ] Optimize for local SEO (keywords, meta, hreflang)
- [ ] Validate brand voice consistency
- [ ] Review by native speaker (recommended)
- [ ] Publish with proper language tags

## Key Frameworks

- **Transcreation vs Translation**: Translation (word-for-word) vs Transcreation (meaning + cultural adaptation)
- **Localization Maturity**: Basic (translate) → Good (adapt) → Excellent (native feel)
- **SEO Localization**: Local keywords, hreflang tags, regional search behavior
- **Brand Voice Preservation**: Maintain brand personality across languages

## Supported Languages

### Tier 1 (High Priority - Full Support)

- **Spanish** (es) - 500M+ speakers
- **French** (fr) - 300M+ speakers
- **German** (de) - 100M+ speakers
- **Portuguese** (pt) - 250M+ speakers
- **Japanese** (ja) - 125M+ speakers
- **Chinese Simplified** (zh-CN) - 1B+ speakers

### Tier 2 (Medium Priority)

- Italian (it), Dutch (nl), Korean (ko), Arabic (ar)

### Tier 3 (Emerging Markets)

- Hindi (hi), Russian (ru), Polish (pl), Turkish (tr)

## Quick Wins

- **Start with Spanish** - Largest non-English market, easier for English speakers
- **Use AI first** - Then post-edit for quality (faster than human-only)
- **Build glossary** - 50-100 key terms for consistency
- **Localize examples** - Change currency, measurements, cultural references
- **Test with native speakers** - Even 1 native reviewer catches 80% of issues
