# Account Tiering Matrix

| Criteria | Tier 1 (Strategic) | Tier 2 (Target) | Tier 3 (Programmatic) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Definition** | Top 50 "Must Win" accounts. High strategic value. | Next 200 accounts. Strong fit, high potential. | 1000+ accounts. Good fit, scalable coverage. |
| **Revenue** | > $1B | $100M - $1B | < $100M |
| **Intent** | High (Active research) | Medium/High | Any |
| **Coverage** | 1:1 (Dedicated AE/SDR pod) | 1:Few (Vertical/Segment owner) | 1:Many (Automated nurture) |
| **Marketing** | Bespoke campaigns, events, direct mail. | Industry/Persona campaigns. | General nurture, webinars. |
| **SLA** | Weekly touchpoints. | Bi-weekly touchpoints. | Monthly/Trigger-based. |

## Scoring Logic (Example)
- **Firmographic (40%)**: Revenue, Employee Count, Industry.
- **Technographic (20%)**: Competitor presence, complementary tech.
- **Intent (30%)**: 6sense/Bombora signals, website visits.
- **Engagement (10%)**: Email opens, event attendance.

**Thresholds:**
- **Tier 1**: Score > 90
- **Tier 2**: Score 70-89
- **Tier 3**: Score 50-69
